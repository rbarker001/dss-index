Built To Withstand Scrutiny

Severity-weighted.
Recency-honest.

Every methodology decision is disclosed, modeled before adoption, and recorded — because this data ends up in due-diligence memos and litigation support, where the method is the first thing challenged.

A surveyor's severity finding counts.

Citations are weighted by the CMS scope-and-severity letter before any threshold applies. One immediate-jeopardy finding classifies a condition as Recognized on its own — mirroring how CMS itself treats immediate jeopardy as categorical, not cumulative.

×1D–F · no harm
×2G–I · actual harm
×3J–L · imm. jeopardy

Recency is shown, not hidden.

Every classification carries its citation timeline. A condition last cited in 2019 and one cited last quarter are both visible as exactly what they are — the reader weighs recency with the dates in hand, not behind a tunable decay constant.
